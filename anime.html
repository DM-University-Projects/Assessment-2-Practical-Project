Must Watch Anime Series

Top Anime's

Show all Action Adventure Slice Of Life

Attack On Titans

Action

Words don't cut it to describe the overwhelming action in this anime. Let's just say that there's a colossal titan. That colossal titan is this anime. Watch it now to see for yourself what extreme action and story we're talking about.

Hunter X Hunter

Action & Adventure

Based on the art style and character design, you're probably thinking this is a fun little series about friendship and believing in yourself. You could not be more wrong.

One Piece

Action, Adventure, Fighting

A swashbuckling adventure featuring duels with supernatural powers, fun characters, voyages and tons of pirates, One Piece is one of the most popular anime series out there. It will certainly fulfill any action craving you have.

FairyTails

Action, Adventure, Fighting

Fairy Tail is a 175 action/fantasy series that packs one hell of a powerful punch. With a plethora of fun characters and epic duels featuring all sorts of magic, Fairy Tail is a great series if you are looking for some good popcorn material!

Claymore

Action, Fighting

In a world rife with deadly creatures called "youma", a young silver eyed woman, Clare, works on behalf of an organisation that trains female youma halfbreeds into warriors with the ability to destroy these creatures. Considered a rogue for picking up a stray child & almost losing herself to her youma side by "Awakening", she is constantly assigned rather dangerous missions.

Bleach

Action, Adventure, Fighting

This anime follows a boy named Ichigo, a gifted kid who happens to be more than just a normal teenager. Bleach is both fast paced and slow at times. But if you can stand the fillers, it always gets back to the story line eventually. The powers/fights in the series are are absolutely amazing, and the humor brakes during episodes always allow a smooth ride.

Naruto Shippuden

Action, Adventure, Fighting

This is truly going to go down as a timeless anime. It has some of the greatest fight scenes of all animes for sure. And as of recently, with the deaths of so many popular characters, you never really know who could win or lose a battle

Cowboy Bebop

Action, Adventure, Fighting

Cowboy Bebop is one of those series that is just impossible to criticize, its a very good anime that deserves to be on this list. A good story and great characters, a must watch if you like action-packed Shonens that don't get boring.

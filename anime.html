Bleach

Action, Adventure

This anime follows a boy named Ichigo, a gifted kid who happens to be more than just a normal teenager. Bleach is both fast paced and slow at times. But you'll never regret the watch.

Naruto Shippuden

Action, Adventure

This is truly a masterpiece. It has some of the greatest fight scenes of all animes for sure. And as of recently, with the deaths of so many popular characters.

Cowboy Bebop

Action, Adventure

Cowboy Bebop is one of those series that is just impossible to criticize, its a very good anime that deserves to be on this list. A good story and great characters, a must watch if you like action-packed Shonens that don't get boring.

Angel Beats

Action, Adventure, Romance, Comedy

Angel Beats is a rare anime able to be both comedy and tearjerker without either feeling out of place in either the world or narrative. The purgatory world, its rules, and its mystery are beautifully handled; and the thematic exploration of teenage life is something any viewer over twenty will be able to identify with. And while the anime does have its weaknesses in the plot structure and characters’ backstories, the chances are you’ll be too busy laughing or crying to notice.

ChottoItsAnime | The Beginning

So ChottoItsAnime Started off in 2012-2013 due to the every so growing love for animes, games and just and overal enjoymnet into the culture of it all. With Anime you learn so much, you hear so less about what it can offer but instead you sometime pick up nagative sides of it and never the positive. So This webiste is here, To offer you the user to see whats its all about, watch what we enjoy and just to be apart of it all. EVEN IF ITS JUST FOR A MOMENT.!!
